FAQ

1.Can you use it with the engine off? If Yes how long will it take before it will kill my cars battery?
How long to Kill (completely discharge) your battery will depend on the size and condition of your battery AND the power load you require from the inverter. For a relatively new, good condition battery. With a load of 50% or max (375 watts) the inverter will draw about 450 watts from your battery. For a typical compact-car battery rated at 35 Ampere/hour (441 watt/hrs) capacity, the battery will be " dead" in less then one hour.
